~~ BIO ~~

Isaac Otto is a composer and improviser based in Los Angeles, CA. Isaac recently graduated from the Integrated Composition, Improvisation and Technology PhD program at UC Irvine where he specialized in 20th- and 21st-century music notations. His dissertation "Scoring the Unknown: Rethinking Fixity and Openness in Western Art Music Notation" is now available via UCI's library and ProQuest.

Isaac specializes in saxophone, clarinet, and bass clarinet improvisation.
